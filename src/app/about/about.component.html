Our Heritage

From Humble Beginnings to Coffee Excellence

Founded in 2012 by master barista Elena Rodriguez, Happy Cafe began as a small corner shop with a big dream. What started as a single espresso machine and twelve seats has grown into a beloved destination for coffee connoisseurs and community members alike.

Elena's journey began in the coffee fields of Colombia, where she developed a deep appreciation for the craft of coffee cultivation. After training with world-renowned baristas in Italy and Japan, she brought her expertise to create a space where coffee is treated as both an art and a science.

Today, we continue Elena's vision by sourcing only the finest specialty-grade beans, roasting in small batches, and training our baristas to the highest standards. Our commitment to quality has earned us recognition as one of the top coffee destinations in the region, featured in "The Art of Coffee" magazine's annual best cafe list for five consecutive years.

Our Core Values

Sustainable Sourcing

We partner directly with ethical farms that prioritize environmental stewardship and fair wages for workers. Our beans are certified by the Rainforest Alliance and we maintain direct trade relationships with 12 family-owned farms across 6 countries. Click to learn more about our sustainability initiatives.

Artisan Craftsmanship

Every drink is prepared with precision and care, honoring traditional techniques while innovating. Our baristas undergo 200 hours of training before serving customers, mastering everything from perfect milk texturing to advanced pour-over methods. Explore our training program details.

Community Connection

We're more than a cafe - we're a gathering place that fosters connection and supports local artists. Each month we host community events, from live jazz nights to barista workshops, and display rotating exhibitions from local artists. See upcoming events.

Our Coffee Philosophy

At Happy Cafe, we believe great coffee is the result of respecting the entire journey - from seed to cup. We meticulously control every variable, from the altitude at which our beans are grown to the precise water temperature used in brewing.

Our rotating single-origin selections highlight the unique terroir of each region, while our signature blends offer consistent excellence. We roast in 10kg batches to ensure peak freshness, with profiles developed specifically for each brewing method.

More than just a beverage, we see coffee as a medium for connection, creativity, and community. Every cup we serve carries this philosophy forward.
